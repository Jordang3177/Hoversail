Guild Policies

Expectations:

  1. Owning up to mistakes - Don't make excuses
  2. Being viable - Raid Utility
  3. Mechanics - Warcraft log "analyzing team"
  4. Numbers - No Grey Parsing. 50-60% minimum with ultimatum to improve
  5. Good Attitude - Self-Critical, Looking to Improve, Personal Accountability
  6. Personal Investment - Know Fights Ahead of Time, Know Guild Strats, Get Required Gear, Read up on changes to your class
  7. Punctuality - Be on 15 minutes Early and Ready to Go. Mention in discord if you will be late or miss

Raid Days and Times:

Hoversail will be raiding on Wednesdays and Thursdays from 9pm-12am EST

Raid Policies

  • Loot Policy - Personal Loot
  • Attendance Policy - Soft 90% with a Spreadsheet that is public to the Guild
  • Consumable Policy - Provided by the Guild, excluding pots. But please be ready for at least one nights worth of raid flask and food just in case

HR Problems

  • Officers led - Talk to them and figure out the problem first
  • Multiple Officers will discuss together to figure out the best solution
  • A Document will be made in order for everyone to be held accountable in the situation and for transparency

Hoversail has a no Poaching Policy

Don't talk to players who are in other guilds unless they reach out to you first or if they are guildless. We want to have a good environment for everyone involved and are not looking to be seen in a negative light to the rest of the community. So please be respectful of others and leave them alone if they have a home already

Application Process

  1. To be accepted or declined based on app via guild email and based on outlined applicable expectations listed above
  2. Trial Process - This will be a two week period in which you will trial on bosses in both farm and progression. Then after this two week period there will be a metting with the officers to look at how you did and see if you meet the expectations required of you listed above. Trials that are not accepted are welcome to saty in the guild and are open to a chance to improve and try again later.
